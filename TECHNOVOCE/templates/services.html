Our Services

Live Sound

Our main service is providing sound technicians for venues and performers who want a professional sound tailored to their requirements. From poetry jams to 'Rat Pack' shows, we have the experience to give your audience exactly the sound you want.

Recording and Production

Our recording services focus on spoken word application, like audiobooks, podcasts and radio plays. We can provide any level of service from simple one-off recordings to fully produced shows with music and foley work. We handle the technical side so you can let your creativity flow!

Consultancy and Installations

Targeted at venues of any size, we can provide advice and support when you decide to install or replace in-house sound and lighting systems. Why guess what you need? We can take the stress out of decision making, and have the industry contacts to help you plan, manage, and complete your installation projects.

Anything Else?

We're happy to help out with other jobs that require technical expertise and an understanding of sound, like acoustic testing and noise surveys. If you're looking for friendly and helpful staff with good technical knowledge, why not drop us a line and disucss your requirements.

Whatever service you're looking for, feel free to get in touch.
